# Manager or Engineer Confirmation Template

This template helps a reviewer provide factual, independent confirmation. The reviewer should use their own words and confirm only facts they personally know.

## Suggested Factual Structure

### 1. Operational problem

Describe the manufacturing translation or knowledge-consistency problem that existed before the platform work.

### 2. Individual contribution

Describe what Aoi Minamoto personally designed, implemented, led, or decided. Distinguish this contribution from routine team activity.

### 3. Technical significance

Explain why the controlled-knowledge architecture, resilient processing, HMI/document workflow, or deployment planning mattered to engineering operations.

### 4. Observed use or impact

State only directly observed and verifiable facts, such as:

- engineering workflows supported
- reviewers or teams involved
- measurable usage or quality results
- reliability improvements
- reduced repeated work
- requests for expansion or reuse

### 5. Leadership and independence

Explain any architecture ownership, cross-functional coordination, problem framing, technical direction, or initiative demonstrated.

### 6. Future relevance

Describe any approved next step, production plan, reuse opportunity, or Yokoten interest that the reviewer directly knows.

## Integrity Guidance

- Avoid generic praise without examples.
- Do not copy language the reviewer cannot independently verify.
- Do not include confidential details in a public version.
- Keep the signed full confirmation in a private evidence package unless publication is explicitly approved.
