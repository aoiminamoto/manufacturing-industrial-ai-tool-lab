# Manufacturing AI Ontology Concept

## Title

Manufacturing AI Ontology Concept  
From Controlled Terminology to Plant AI Readiness

## Core Message

In manufacturing, AI is only as good as the plant knowledge foundation it can understand and trust.

## Concept

This ontology concept describes a governed semantic foundation for plant AI. It connects manufacturing terminology, PLC/HMI comments, NG codes, alarms, signals, process context, and maintenance actions so AI systems can interpret plant context more reliably.

## Foundation

```text
Base Layer
Controlled manufacturing semantics for plant AI
```

## Governance

- Approved terms
- Owners
- Version control
- Source traceability
- Review status

## Semantic Objects

- Machine terms
- Process terms
- PLC/HMI comments
- Alarms and signals
- NG codes
- Maintenance actions

## AI Outcomes

- Translation quality
- Terminology consistency
- Troubleshooting context
- Plant AI readiness

## Positioning

The JP-EN translation tool is the first practical application of this broader manufacturing AI ontology concept. The long-term goal is not only translation, but a governed knowledge foundation that supports future plant AI systems.
